surviving the microverse portal 2021

Surving the Portal is an annual forum that helps students accepted into Microverse understand what to expect during the first few weeks in the program. Making the decision to pursue a career in Software development can be a tough one, but choosing to start your journey as a student in Microverse may seem even tougher. Every year a selected few, out of millions of applicants, are accepted into the Microverse online Program, this journey, for some can be really daunting. The first few weeks, usually called THE PORTAL, can take it's toll on students making the switch into Software development. In this forum Micronauts who have just passed through "THE PORTAL" shall be discussing the challanges they faced and how they managed them.
